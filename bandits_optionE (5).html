You and another bandit have escaped to the mountains
How to play
Each night, both players secretly pick one action.
Actions resolve simultaneously.
How to win
The first to make 20k wins.
Buried treasure doesn't count until dug up.
Actions
Add
Doubles the loot. Adds $1K to an empty pot.
Steal
Transfers the loot to your hand.
Raid
Transfers your opponent's hand to your hand.
Rob
Adds $1,000 to your hand.
Spy
Catch a Stash or Dig. Take the treasure to your hand. Two Spies swap vaults.
Stash
Buries your hand. Buried treasure is safe from Raid & Ambush.
Dig
Unearths buried treasure to your hand.
Guard
Defends against Steal. Two Guards split the loot.
Ambush
Defends and reverses Raid & Spy.
Resolution order
ADD→ STASH→ ROB→ RAID→ DIG→ SPY→ STEAL→ GUARD→ AMBUSH
